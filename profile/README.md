## 🌟 Our Story

We are a husband‑and‑wife team — an **AI Engineer** and a **UX Designer** — raising three wonderful kids as immigrants. Every person in our home of five shares one magical letter: **J**. That’s where the name comes from.

> **JFive = 5 J’s, one family, one shared dream.**
>
> We know what it’s like to juggle a new language, a new country, school pickups, bedtime stories, and a career — all in the same day. So we decided to turn that lived experience into products.
>
> ## 🚀 What We Build
>
> We design and ship **AI‑powered tools** that feel less like software and more like a helpful friend in your pocket:
>
> -  📚  **English, together** — bite‑sized AI practice parents and kids can do side by side.
> -  -  🗓️  **Family Time OS** — smart schedules that respect real family chaos.
>    -  -  💵  **Side‑income Copilot** — AI workflows that help parents earn on their own hours.
>       -  -  🧠  **Calm & Clarity** — gentle routines, reminders, and mental load relief.
>
